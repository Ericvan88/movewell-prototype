# Project instructions

- Never use long dashes (em dash "—" or en dash "–") anywhere: not in chat, not in UI copy, not in labels or notes.
- Do not use "-" as sentence punctuation either. Hyphens are fine inside compound words (e.g. "sensory-calm", "drop-off").
- Rewrite around dashes: use a period, comma, colon, or "·" separators in metadata lines instead.
